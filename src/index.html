About Us

We are eight senior students from The Academy for Math, Science, and Engineering, a high school located in Rockaway, NJ. As we are passionate about conducting research and learning about various fields of engineering, we decided to collaborate on a huge research project, which has been condensed into this set of papers. We thank Gao Jun Wu, PhD Candidate and member of the Stanford FPAL Lab, for contributing his indispensable technical expertise.

Overview

This is a set of three papers on drone propellers, optimization of their aeroacoustic performance, design standards, and 3D printing methodology.


Paper 1

Acoustic Analysis of Propeller Annoyance Using Uneven Blade Spacing and Toroidal Geometry
